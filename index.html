Products

Ethiopia is one of the biggest providers of Coffee Arabica to the global market. Ethiopian coffee is globally recognized and loved for it's organic nature and unique flavor.
Generally, Ethiopian coffee is prepared and supplied to the market as washed and natural which passes through different preparation steps. We offer both the washed and naturally processed coffee. Natural processing gives the coffee a strong fruity and wine flavor, while Wet processing gives it a consistent flavor, a process commonly used for top premium coffees.
What’s more? The quality of Ethiopian coffee is categorized based on location. Bereka Coffee can be traced back to the specific farm areas that help supply coffee beans with consistent quality. Our second specialization is on supplying fermented coffee.
