Nekemte

Nekemte coffee is grown in Western Ethiopia and is prized for its pleasant fruity flavor. Higher-grade coffee can sometimes be distinctive, with honey, citrus, and floral notes. It has long, elongated beans that range in color from green to brownish. The cup is generally clean, with a light fruity acidic flavor and the body of a high-quality mocha. It is an important component of a specialty roaster's blend, but it also makes an excellent gourmet single.
